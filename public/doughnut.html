Before you start a social business

The Mission

The objective of this section is to help you write a succinct mission statement that describes your social business\'s purpose.

Motivation

Ask yourself, “Why do I want to start up this social business?” If it is for personal financial gain then you should consider setting up a sole trader business where you own it and take the profit from it.
